# Retinue Instructors

This mod allows members of your retinue to teach your brothers new perks.

By hiring retinue members, all your bros get access to specific perks depending
on the retinue member.

## Technical Notes

See a way to add perks to the perk tree at https://github.com/Battle-Modders/mod-reforged/blob/6555f1c0838d1d874284279dea5a38480267d1bd/mod_reforged/hooks/scenarios/world/rangers_scenario.nut#L16
